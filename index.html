Chester Bennington's Early Life

  • 1976.3.20 - Born in Phoenix, Ariz. His mother was a nurse, while his father was a police detective. He showed great interest in music at a young age and dreamed of becoming a member of Stone Temple Pilots, which he later achieved.
  • 1983 - Suffered sexual abuse from an older male friend for 6 years. He later revealed the abuser's identity to his father, but chose not to continue the case after he realized the abuser was a victim himself.
  • 1987 - His parents divorced and his father gained custody of him. After the divorce, Bennington started abusing drugs. But he was able to overcome his drug addiction eventually by his efforts and other people's help.
  • 1992 - Was physically bullied in high school. "I was knocked around like a rag doll at school, for being skinny and looking different.", he said in an interview.
  • 1993 - Worked at different shops before starting his career as a professional musician.
  • 1996 - Married his first wife, Samantha Marie Olit. The marriage maintained for 9 years. He married Talinda Ann Bentley in 2006.
